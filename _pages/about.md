# üòÄ Biography

I am currently an **Associate Professor** at the School of Cyber Science and Engineering, **Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST)**. I am a member of the **Security Pride** Research Group (**Security**, **Pri**vacy, and **De**pendability in Emerging Software Systems), where I conduct research in collaboration with Prof. [Haoyu Wang](https://howiepku.github.io/). From August 2018 to November 2020, I conducted **Postdoctoral research** at the **Shenzhen Institute of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences**. Before joining academia in 2023, I worked as a Senior Security Researcher at **Sangfor Technologies** for five years, gaining extensive industrial experience. I received my PhD degree in Computer Science from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in 2018.  

I am interested in the intersection of security, program analysis, and AI systems.
Specifically, I focus on developing impactful, real-world solutions for detecting and defending against vulnerabilities in a wide range of systems, including Open-Source Software Supply Chains, Cross-Platform Endpoints, and Agentic Systems.
- **OSS Security**: NPM/PyPI Code Poisoning (ASE'23, ASE'24)
- **Endpoint Security**: Ransomware (CCS'24), MiniApp Security (ASE'23, ASE'25) & Privacy (SaTS'23, TOSEM'25)
- **Agentic Security**: LLM Supply Chain (ASE'24, TOSEM'25), Agentic Software (ICSE'26), LLM Infra (ASE'25, ICSE'26)

I am actively looking for self-motivated Ph.D. and Master‚Äôs students to join my team. If you are passionate about security research, please email me with your CV.
